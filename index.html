Nico Stephou (1990) is a Cypriot graphic designer, art director and electronic music producer based in Berlin. He has studied in Gerrit Rietveld Academy of Fine Arts and Design in Amsterdam. He is the co-founder of the collaboration platform and record label Honest Electronics and the yearly festival “The Gathering”. As an Art Director he has created identities for multiple foundations, record labels, art projects and publications.
