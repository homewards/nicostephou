Nico Stephou is a graphic designer, art director and electronic music producer based in Berlin. He is the co-founder of the community platform and record label Honest Electronics and the yearly festival “The Gathering” in Cyprus. As an art director he has created identities for multiple foundations, record labels, art projects and publications.
